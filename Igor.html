Vaquita


Vaquitas are small porpoises native to the gulf of california in mexico. They have been hunted because their bladders were believed to have healing properies.
There are now less than 20 individuals left in the world there have been attempts to save the species but the odds are not in their favor
There is not enough genetic diversity for them to make a come back. They were discovered in 1958 but by then we had already killed many off.
